What I learned from my two year missionary service
  1. Heavenly Father loves us and wants a relationship with us
    • Our Heavenly Father loved us so deeply that He gave His only begotten Son to die for us
    • The Restoration is a restoring of us to God by means of a Church and organized priesthood power
    • God will do everything in His power to make all blessings available to us
  2. Jesus Christ can be your best friend
    • The only way to come to know Jesus as our Savior is through the Spirit
    • The Spirit is the same yesterday, today and forever
    • Which means that we can know Him just as well, if not better, than the Apostles of old knew Him
  3. Our purpose is change
    • Amidst so many contradicting voices in the world, the Gospel provides us an anchor for the soul
    • We have come to earth to become like our Heavenly Father through the grace of His only Begotten
    • Our experiences, choices and trials are our figurtive refiners fire in this life, Christ being the Refiner
